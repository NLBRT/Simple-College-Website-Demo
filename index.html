Welcome to Siliguri Government Polytechnic!

Siliguri Government Polytechnic since its foundation in 1998 has been playing a distinct role in the field of Engineering. It is situated in Siliguri, a city under Darjeeling District in West Bengal. It is a Government institute which offers only diploma courses. The courses offered by SGP are approved by All India Council for Technical Education and the college is affiliated to West Bengal Council of Technical Education. The institute is setup with an elaborate infrastructure with an eco friendly campus. It comprises conference room, language lab, well-equipped laboratories, large classrooms, modern teaching equipment, a well stocked library, workshop and different accommodations for boys (1.5 acres) and girls (1.38 acres, staff quarters included).
